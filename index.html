    About Us

    Edutech is a website application that focuses on using technology to solve problems in the Education sector by helping with Database Managment of schools. Due to its flexibility, it can be geared towards meeting the immediate need of the school and making learning easy for students with our EduTech E-Learning platform

    Our Aim

    To ensure the safety of all academic documents and also provide access to interactive learning to school children.

    Our Vision

    To create a safe and secure platform for data storage thereby helping with easing the stress of Teachers, giving students easy access to their educational data. At EDUTECH, safety and accuracy is our watch-word.
